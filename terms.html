  2. Eligibility
    1. In order to use the Services, You need to register and maintain a User Account on the Site and You must:
      1. be a natural person,
      2. be at least 18 years of age if You want to deposit funds from Your bank account,
      3. submit Your email address, and
      4. comply with these Terms at all times.
    2. Officers, directors and agents of Challengermode and its affiliates are not eligible to participate in any Competitions, other than for test and product development purposes.
    3. Challengermode may at any time require You to provide proof of eligibility to allow You access to the Site. Failure to provide an evidence of identification and/or eligibility satisfactory to Challengermode, in its sole and absolute discretion, shall result in the suspension and/or termination of such User Account. For User Accounts that are terminated due to non-eligibility, Credits previously deposited to the User Account will be refunded to the account holder.
    4. You are fully aware of the fact that Credits paid to participate in Pay-to-enter Competitions and Tournaments are non-refundable.
  3. Fees
    1. To participate in certain Matches or Tournaments You need to pay a fee. The fees applied for participating in these Matches or Tournaments are;
      1. Prize commission to Challengermode according to Section 11.1;
      2. a transaction fee and/or an administration fee to Challengermode when You withdraw Credits according to Section 6.5.2; and
      3. currency converter fee to Payment Provider when paying with another currency than EUR according to Section 11.3.
    2. If You do not pay the fee, You will not be able to participate in the Matches and Tournaments which requires a fee to be paid.
